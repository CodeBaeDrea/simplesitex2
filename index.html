About US

Beyond being the best thing since sliced bread, we are a community software engineers, some old, some new, that have gathered to teach, learn, and grow. At a time when the world was crumbling around us, The 2019 Pandemic wasn't the only thing taken by storm, 100Devs was born. Created to elevate 100 persons in to a employable software engineer in 30 weeks. Geared towards black and brown individuals. Aiming towards success is top priority. Taught by none other than Philly's Finest, the self-less, smart, and talented, black brother, Leon Noel. Manager Director for Resilient Coders, a Distinguished Faculty Member at General Assembly, public speaker and teacher who has taught at Harvard and MIT to name a few who has assisted with 85% graduates, with no prior experience, get full time offers of average salaries starting at $98,000.
